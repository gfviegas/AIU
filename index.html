Assync Image Uploader or simply, AIU!

This jQuery + AJAX + PHP plugin uploads an image asynchronously to your FTP server and just load it in your page. That's right! No reloads!! You can send and show your image without refresh!!

To instructions, suggestions and more about the AIU, check the "Read Me" file in Git-Hub.

Send your image

Max 1MB size. Accepted extensions: jpeg, jpg, gif, bmp and png
